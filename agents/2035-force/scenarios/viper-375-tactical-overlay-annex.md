# Viper-375 Tactical Overlay Annex

> Scenario use only.
>
> This annex is **not baseline doctrine** and should be used only as a scenario overlay.

## Annex Purpose
This annex defines the scenario-specific tactical overlay for the Viper-375 battlespace. It is intended to support planning, briefing, mission design, and integration with CAMD operational products while remaining separate from baseline doctrine files.

---

## AO Summary
- **AO Name:** Viper-375
- **Battlespace Size:** 15 km × 25 km
- **Orientation:** Portrait
- **Grid:** 500-meter grid
- **Operational Character:** Mixed mountain, river, lake, forest, farmland, and urban terrain with multiple maneuver corridors.
- **Scenario Context:** OPFOR controls dominant northern terrain while BLUFOR advances from the south through three primary axes.

## Overlay Design Guidance
- Use minimalist ARMA-style markers and clean tactical symbology.
- Preserve terrain readability over all overlay elements.
- Keep linework thin, high-contrast, and uncluttered.
- Avoid overcrowding the map with labels or repeated symbols.
- Prioritize maneuver understanding first, symbolic density second.
