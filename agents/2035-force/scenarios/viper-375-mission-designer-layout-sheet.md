# Viper-375 Tactical Map Mission-Designer Layout Sheet

> Scenario use only.
>
> This annex is **not baseline doctrine** and should be used only as a scenario/map-design support file.

## Map Format
- **Title:** Viper-375 Tactical Map
- **Orientation:** Portrait
- **Dimensions:** 15 km (west-east) × 25 km (south-north)
- **Grid:** 500-meter grid
- **Reference System:** Origin at southwest corner
  - **X-axis:** 0.0 km to 15.0 km west-east
  - **Y-axis:** 0.0 km to 25.0 km south-north

## Terrain Placement Plan

### 1. Northwest Mountain Range
- **Primary footprint:** X 0.5–5.5 / Y 18.0–25.0
- **Foothills extension:** X 4.5–7.0 / Y 14.5–20.0
- **Function:** Observation dominance, restrictive terrain, ridgeline strongpoints, valley shaping.

### 2. Central Lake
- **Footprint:** X 6.0–9.0 / Y 11.0–14.5
- **Shape:** Irregular north-south oval with marshy western edge.
- **Function:** Splits central maneuver space and forces crossing decisions.

### 3. Southeast Lake
- **Footprint:** X 10.5–13.5 / Y 4.0–7.0
- **Shape:** Slightly smaller than central lake, broader east-west spread.
- **Function:** Constrains southeastern movement and creates a protected flank zone.

### 4. North-South River Spine
- **General course:** enters at X 7.8 / Y 25.0 and exits at X 8.6 / Y 0.0
- **Channel width:** 80–200 meters depending on sector.
- **Major crossings:**
  - **Bridge A:** X 7.6 / Y 19.2
  - **Bridge B:** X 8.2 / Y 12.8
  - **Bridge C:** X 8.7 / Y 8.1
  - **Rural crossing:** X 8.9 / Y 5.4
- **Function:** Mobility divider and control line through the center of the AO.

### 5. Southwest Rolling Hills
- **Footprint:** X 1.0–5.5 / Y 2.0–8.5
- **Function:** Covered southern staging area, masked maneuver, hull-down movement.

### 6. Major Forest Zones
- **Forest A (west-central):** X 2.0–5.5 / Y 10.0–15.5
- **Forest B (central-east):** X 8.8–12.5 / Y 10.5–16.0
- **Forest C (south-central/east):** X 6.0–10.5 / Y 2.0–6.5
- **Function:** CP concealment, recon screening, ambush lanes, ISR masking.

### 7. Four Long Valleys / Maneuver Corridors
- **Valley 1 (NW descent corridor):** X 4.5–6.5 / Y 16.0–23.0
- **Valley 2 (central river corridor):** X 7.0–9.5 / Y 7.0–18.5
- **Valley 3 (western approach corridor):** X 3.0–5.0 / Y 6.5–16.0
- **Valley 4 (eastern open corridor):** X 10.5–13.5 / Y 7.0–17.5
- **Function:** Primary maneuver channels for recon, mech, and logistics movement.

### 8. Farmland Zones
- **Farmland Belt South:** X 5.5–11.0 / Y 0.5–5.5
- **Farmland Belt Central:** X 5.0–12.5 / Y 7.0–11.0
- **Farmland Belt Northeast:** X 10.0–14.5 / Y 17.0–22.5
- **Function:** Open exposure zones with roads, hedgerows, and field boundaries.

## Design Rules
- Maintain clean ARMA-style overlays.
- Use minimalist military symbols only.
- Keep labels sparse and highly readable.
- Preserve open terrain readability beneath the 500 m grid.
- Do not over-symbolize towns or infrastructure.
- Terrain should drive maneuver visually before overlays are read.
