# 2035 CAMD Tactical Overlay Annex — Issue #2

## Annex Purpose
This annex translates the Viper-375 Tactical Map into a CAMD operational overlay package suitable for planning, briefing, and integration with the CAMD doctrine set.

---

## AO Summary
- **AO Name:** Viper-375
- **Battlespace Size:** 15 km × 25 km
- **Orientation:** Portrait
- **Operational Character:** Mixed terrain with mountain mass, river spine, dual lakes, forest belts, farmland exposure zones, and multiple maneuver corridors.
- **Enemy Disposition:** OPFOR controls dominant northern terrain, with strongpoints covering central approaches and northeastern access routes.
